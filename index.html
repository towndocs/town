Plot Commands

Spawn Tools Required
Equips the F3X toolset to your local character.
!bt / !btools
Create Plot
Creates a new plot directly where the player is.
!pc / !plotcreate
Rotation
Rotates the active plot orientation. Must clear plot first.
!rotateplot / !rtp
Clear Plot Save First
Deletes all parts on your plot without deleting the plot.
!clearplot / !clp
Remove Plot
Unregisters current plot claim from the server.
!removeplot / !rmp
Storage
Opens the plot storage interface for saving.
!sg / !savegui
Plot Trust
Allows another player to modify your plot.
!pt / !ptrust [player]
Plot Position
Shows the position of your current plot.
!plotposition / !pp
Clear Outside Parts Use Carefully
Deletes parts that glitched outside your plot. May break builds.
!clearoutsideparts / !cop
Quick Save
Saves the plot without opening save GUI. Do !uwc before using.
!ns [Plot Name]

F3X Keybindings

Move Z
Axis translation. Suggested increment: 0.05.
Resize X
Object scaling. Suggested increment: 0.05.
Rotate C
Angular manipulation of parts.
Create J
Spawn new primitive geometry instances.
Paint V
Apply Color3 properties to the selection.
Material N
Apply material textures to the selection.
Physics K
Toggle CanCollide state for selections.
Surface G
Map IDs to specific object faces.
Mesh H
Import external MeshId data.
Light L
Instance Point, Spot, or Surface lights.

Loadout Commands

Creating Weapon & Tool Spawning Loadouts
These commands help you naturally spawn with your preferred weapons without having to spawn everything back again.
Set Spawn
Spawns with ready loadout. Example: !sts SAS+Hunting+Redlaser ballisticsvest balaclava
!sts / !setspawn
Set Armor
Spawns you with the armor you want. Example: !sta riot
!sta [armorname]

VIP Server Commands

Assault Rifles

Assault rifles are the most flexible class of weapon in-game, dominating medium-short range combat and balancing range, damage, and fire rate.

Battle Rifles

Battle Rifles fire fully powered cartridges for maximum stopping power at the cost of recoil and fire rate.

Designated Marksman Rifles

DMRs serve as an in-between for assault rifles and sniper rifles, offering forgiving fire rate with precision.

Sniper Rifles

High range and damage dominate long-range field. Low fire-rate and slow reload. Aim sway present.

Light Machine Guns

Heavy automatic weapons with high magazine capacity for sustained fire at the cost of mobility.

Submachine Guns

Pistol-caliber weapons with high fire rates and excellent mobility. Excel in close quarters.

Shotguns

Trade accuracy and range for sheer stopping power. Can penetrate riot shields.

Handguns

Secondary weapons that are light and concealable. Can be dual-wielded. Revolvers included.

Melee Weapons

Very low range but don't use ammo and ignore armor. Can hit through walls.
